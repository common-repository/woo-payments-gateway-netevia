== Description ==

Simplify the Integration and acceptance of all major payment methods in WooCommerce, wherever you need them. As easy as drag and drop into your WooCommerce webshop with this latest plugin by Netevia. Netevia is committed to simplifying the payment processing for WooCommerce.

Cut out the need to spend long hours on unnecessary paperwork or complicated security compliance procedures. Increase your conversion rate and therefore your revenue by accepting any payment method your customers want to use, while at the same time keeping their information secure. We streamlined, simplified and kept secure the entire payment process for both merchants and their customers.

= Payment methods =
* Credit cards:
* Visa
* MasterCard
* American Express
* Discover (Including Paypal)
* JCB

Please visit the signup page to create a new Netevia account and start accepting payments in no time. Contact info@netevia.com if you have any questions or concerns in regards to this plugin.

No setup, monthly, or hidden fees, pay only for what you use and
Receive your funds to your bank account the same day with our Fast Pass Funding service.

= Features =
* Support for all available Mollie payment methods
* Transparent pricing. No startup fees, no monthly fees, and no gateway fees. No hidden fees. Pay only for what you use.
* Support for full and partial payment refunds (WooCommerce 2.2+)
* Configurable pay outs: daily, weekly, monthly – whatever your preference is.
* Reliable in-house support. You will always be helped by someone who’s an expert in our products.
* Sandbox environment for integration test.
* Create an account in less than 1 minute.
* Powerful dashboard to easily keep track of your payments.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/netevia-payments-for-woocomerce` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Get your App ID and API Key here: https://dashboard.netevia.com/sandbox/integration/byapi#h1_api_reference
4. Use the Settings->WooCommerce->Settings->Payments->Netevia screen to configure the plugin.
5. Enter your App ID and API Key.
